Healthy Turkey Sliders

Recipe Information

  • Prep Time: 15 minutes
  • Cook Time: 20 minutes
  • Total Time: 35 minutes
  • Yield: 14 Sliders
  • Category: Appetizer
  • Method: Stove
  • Cuisine: North American

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs ground turkey
  • 2 eggs, large
  • 1/2 small onion, minced
  • 1 garlic clove, grated
  • 3/4 tsp salt
  • Ground black pepper, to taste
  • 4 pieces of bacon*
  • 1 head of butter lettuce, rinsed and separated*
  • 14 Hawaiaan Buns
  • 1 small red onion, sliced*
  • 3 oz cheese, cut into 14 small slices
  • Ketchup and Mustard*
  • *Optional Ingredients

Steps

Making the Sliders

#1 - Prepare the Turkey Meat:
In a medium bowl, add turkey, eggs, onion, garlic, salt and pepper; mix well with your hands. Set aside. Prepare other ingredients â€“ it really helps to assemble sliders faster and serve warm.
#2 - Prepare the Stove:
Preheat large ceramic non-stick skillet on medium-high heat and cook bacon. Transfer to a cutting board draining fat well into the skillet (we will fry patties in it), then cutting it into 14 pieces.
#3 - Cook the Patties:
Using 1/4 cup measuring cup, scoop meat mixture and form a patty 2.5-3 inch in diameter. Place in a skillet and repeat with the rest. Cover and cook for about 4 minutes, flip over and cook uncovered another 3-4 minutes.
#4 - Place Cheese on Patties (Optional):
Place all patties in a skillet, top with a slice of cheese, cover and let cheese melt on low, about 4-5 minutes.
#5 - Assemble Turkey Sliders:
  • If using Butter Lettuce:
    • Take 2 pieces of lettuce, overlap their ends and form a pocket.
    • Place turkey patty inside, top with your desired toppings.
    • Fold lettuce on top. Stick a long wooden pick in the middle to hold the slider together.
    • Enjoy your slider!
  • If using Hawaiian Bread:
    • Take one hawaiian bun and unfold it
    • Place turkey patty inside, top with your desired toppings.
    • Stick a long wooden pick in the middle to hold the slider together.
    • Enjoy your slider!
